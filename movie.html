The Departed



Two just-graduated officers from Massachusetts State Police Academy follow opposite sides of the law:
Billy Costigan is assigned to work undercover with the Irish mobster Frank Costello to get evidence
to arrest him. His true identity is known only by his superiors Dignam and Oliver Queenan. The protege of Costello,
Colin Sullivan, is promoted in the Massachusetts State Police and is the informer of Costello.
Each police officer gives his best effort trying to disclose the identity of the other "rat."
